News & Update
We'll take care of you
AMSTrust was incorporated on 23 April 2013 and has received its licence to operate Labuan Trust Companies Business from Labuan Financial Services Authority (Labuan FSA) on 7 June 2013.

Our philosophy is to provide our Clients with high quality, value added and comprehensive cost-effective services. We strive for objectivity, flexibility, responsiveness and commercial realism in advising and meeting Clients’ needs.

Our team is committed and attuned to understanding each of our Clients’ business, industry and individual goals. We recognise that the needs of Clients cannot always be met solely by traditional response; instead they require innovative and responsive solutions which advance the Clients’ business as well as their objectives.

We place special emphasis on teamwork in providing an integrated approach to deal with the business problems of our Clients. Our team with close consultation among each other ensures that full range of the Company’s expertise and inter disciplinary capabilities are provided to Clients
